Executive Summary

Most AI and analytics portfolios were scaled before power and latency realities caught up. The result: stranded capacity, hidden waste, and fragile rollouts. The AI-Ready Data Center & FinOps Audit compresses months of guesswork into 4–6 weeks: a defensible power/latency budget, right-sizing plan, and an executive-ready roadmap your team can execute.

Who It’s For

  • CIO/CTO
  • Heads of Platform/Infra
  • IT Finance/FinOps
  • AI/Analytics Owners

Outcomes

  • Power & latency budget mapped to target workloads and facilities.
  • Cost visibility & waste opportunities (typical targets 15–30% waste identified).
  • Placement & migration plan with guardrails, SLOs, and sequencing.

Detailed Deliverables

  • Asset inventory & taxonomy (compute, storage, network, accelerators).
  • Baseline pack: cost ledger, utilization heatmap, power/latency snapshot.
  • Right-sizing report: instance/class recommendations, reservation strategy, GPU queue policy.
  • FinOps guardrails: tagging, budgets, anomaly alerts, showback.
  • Placement matrix: on-prem/colo/cloud blend with constraints and tradeoffs.
  • Risk register: dependencies, mitigations, owners, decision log.
  • Migration runbook: phases, pre-checks, roll-back steps.
  • Executive decision deck: options, impact, and 90-day plan.

Method & Timeline (4–6 weeks)

  • Week 1 – Discovery & baseline
  • Weeks 2–3 – Right-sizing & guardrails
  • Weeks 3–4 – Placement & migration plan
  • Weeks 5–6 – Executive readout & 90-day plan

Sample Artifacts

  • Heatmap: utilization vs. cost by workload class
  • Matrix: workload → power/latency target → placement
  • Checklist: migration pre-checks & back-out
