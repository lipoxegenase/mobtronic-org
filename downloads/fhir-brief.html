FHIR/TEFCA 90-Day Sprint

Pass audit. Reduce admin burden. Prove it with evidence.

Executive Summary

Regulatory timelines won’t wait, and “compliant” doesn’t always mean “usable.” The FHIR/TEFCA 90-Day Sprint stands up priority endpoints, stitches in the operations that make them stick, and compiles an evidence pack auditors accept.

Who It’s For

  • Provider IT
  • Payer IT
  • Revenue Cycle
  • Compliance/Privacy

Outcomes

  • Endpoint readiness for priority use cases (e.g., prior auth, patient access).
  • Operational flow with roles/queues/SLAs to keep work moving.
  • Audit evidence (conformance tests, logs, SOPs).

Detailed Deliverables

  • Gap analysis: current endpoints, data mappings, priority use cases.
  • Security model: auth, access, audit, and minimum necessary.
  • Data flows: sequence diagrams, error handling, retries.
  • Validation suite: synthetic data, conformance tests, result logs.
  • Operational runbook: roles, escalations, SLAs, dashboards.
  • Evidence packet: conformance results, SOPs, screenshots, change log.
  • Training & handoff: quick-start guides and admin training.

Method & Timeline (12 weeks)

  • Weeks 1–2: Gap report, risk register, plan.
  • Weeks 3–6: Endpoint enablement, security & flows.
  • Weeks 7–10: Validation & fixes.
  • Weeks 11–12: Evidence packet, training, cutover.

Sample Artifacts

  • Endpoint catalog with scopes and contacts
  • Test plan with synthetic data sets
  • SOP for change management and key rotations
